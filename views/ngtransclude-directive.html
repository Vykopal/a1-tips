ngTransclude directive

Purpose: enables you to embed a portion of DOM from the outer environment to your directive and access the scope of the embedded code

Simple transclusion example

The "original" directive content is cadet blue color. The transcluded portion od DOM is bisque color.

Look how we managed to bind together the two controllers - parent controller ngt and the directive controller via binding to property search-exp

Notice that both parent controller and directive use the "controllerAs" pattern and communicate via explicit binding, which prevents scope inheritance mess.
